About the University

Established in 2016 under the JSS Mahavidyapeetha, JSS Science and Technology University is the second university established by the group in Mysuru.

Our Vision

To be an international leader in engineering education, research, and application of knowledge to benefit society globally.

Key Features

  • 100+ Acres sprawling lush green campus.
  • Focus on Outcome Based Education (OBE).
  • Strong industry-institute interaction cells.
